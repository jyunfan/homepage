ATAK (Android Team Awareness Kit) is a situational-awareness platform used by military and civil teams. It provide real-time mapping, location sharing, mission planning, sensor integration, and plugin extensibility. ATAK helps team coordinate safely and efficiently during complex operations on Android devices.
